Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the ProrokLab, at the University of Cambridge. I obtained a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Systems Engineering from the Universidad de Zaragoza in 2025, supervised by Prof. Eduardo Montijano and Prof. Carlos Sagüés. I visited Prof. Nikolay A. Atanasov at the University of California, San Diego, in 2022 and 2024. I am a Fulbright Scholar, a DAAD AInet Fellow and a Research Associate of Corpus Christi college.

Research Interests

Robotics, networked system, control, learning and, occasionally, power electronics.
