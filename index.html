Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the ProrokLab, in the Department of Computer Science and Technology of the University of Cambridge. I obtained a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Systems Engineering from the Universidad de Zaragoza, advised by Prof. Eduardo Montijano and Prof. Carlos Sagüés. I have been a visiting scholar at the Existential Robotics Laboratory, supervised by Prof. Nikolay A. Atanasov, from April-2022 to August-2022 and from May-2024 to November-2024. I am a Fulbright Scholar and a DAAD AInet Fellow.

Research Interests

I'm open to any amazing topic related to robotics, networked system, control and learning, and, occasionally, power electronics.
